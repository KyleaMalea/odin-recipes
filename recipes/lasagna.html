Lasagna

Description

A Delicious Lasagna packed with protein and easy for meal prepping. Filled with a lot of meat and cheese and will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Two Pounds of Ground Beef (96%)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • 68OG Vodka Pasta Sauce
  • Barilla Lasagne Oven Ready Pasta
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Fat Free Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Put the Ground Beef in a pan and cook that until almost brown.
  3. Add Vodka Sauce, Salt, Pepper, and Smoke Paprika.
  4. Grab a baking pan and spray with cooking oil.
  5. Add a layer of Ground Beef to the Bottom.
  6. Add a layer of uncooked pasta on top of the meat.
  7. Add the ricotta cheese on top of the pasta.
  8. 4 ounces of spinach and 4 ounces of mushroom on top of the ricotta cheese.
  9. Add Mozzarella Cheese on top of the spinach and Mushrooms.
  10. Add one more layer of each.
  11. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Nutritional Facts

  • 6 servings
  • 488 calories
  • 65g Protein
  • 30g Carbs
  • 11g Fat
